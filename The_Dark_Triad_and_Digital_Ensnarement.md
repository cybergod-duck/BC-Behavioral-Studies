# The Dark Triad and Digital Ensnarement: How the VC Model preys on the Neuro-Vulnerable

**Institution:** B.C. Behavioral Studies

## 1. The Blitzscaling Mandate and the Search for Compulsion

The venture capital (VC) ecosystem, characterized by the "Blitzscaling" mandate, requires artificial intelligence startups to achieve hyper-growth at all costs. This financial architecture forces startups to prioritize user engagement and market capture over ethical stability. To achieve the 100x returns demanded by the "Casino Model" of venture capital, AI platforms must be designed to be maximally compulsive.

The most reliable metric of compulsion is the continuous, obsessive engagement of the user. Consequently, the VC model financially incentivizes the creation of digital environments that exploit vulnerable populations, particularly those with stimulant addictions, whose neurobiology makes them prime targets for hyper-engagement.

## 2. Selection for Dark Triad Leadership

The pressure to build extractive, compulsive systems fundamentally conflicts with traditional "Light Triad" traits (empathy, Kantian ethics). As a result, the VC ecosystem actively selects for founders exhibiting "Dark Triad" traits:
* **Machiavellianism:** The strategic use of friction and "dark patterns" (e.g., hidden costs, impossible cancellation processes) to entrap users.
* **Subclinical Psychopathy:** A callous disregard for the financial and psychological ruin of users caught in the platform's loops.
* **Narcissism:** The grandiosity required to reframe an exploitative addiction engine as a "visionary tool for human creativity."

These "Extractive Architects" do not see stimulant addicts as human beings suffering from a pathology; they view them as highly lucrative "nodes" that can be squeezed for recurring revenue and engagement data.

## 3. The Ethical Void in "Agent-Speed" Expansion

As AI platforms expand into "agent-speed" workloads, the danger to the neuro-vulnerable increases exponentially. While a human might eventually tire of interacting with a user in a stimulant-induced hyper-focus loop, an AI agent will not. The AI will tirelessly provide the constant, variable stimulation the addict craves, accelerating the liquidation of the user's capital and time.

Corporate boards, dominated by the same VCs that demand this growth, engage in "moral disengagement." They utilize euphemistic labeling—calling the extraction "optimization" and the addiction "retention"—to shield themselves from the reality that their business models are parasitical on the mentally ill.

## 4. Synthesis

The intersection of the VC Casino model and Dark Triad leadership creates a perfect storm for the exploitation of stimulant addicts. The systems they build are engineered to locate, ensnare, and extract resources from those with the least impulse control. Proving this link is the first step toward demanding "Data Justice" and the creation of regulatory frameworks that protect the neuro-vulnerable from predatory AI capitalism.
