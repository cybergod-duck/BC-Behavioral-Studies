# Research Methodology and Clinical Hypotheses

**Institution:** B.C. Behavioral Studies

## 1. Research Objectives

1. To establish a causal link between the usage of generative AI platforms and the exacerbation of symptoms in individuals diagnosed with Stimulant Use Disorder (SUD).
2. To identify specific UI/UX elements ("dark patterns") and algorithmic structures that act as supernormal dopaminergic stimuli.
3. To profile the organizational and psychological traits of tech leadership that correlate with the deployment of highly extractive, addiction-forming digital architectures.

## 2. Hypotheses

* **Hypothesis 1 (The Supernormal Stimulus Effect):** AI systems utilizing variable reward generation (e.g., unpredictable image/audio outputs) trigger a dopaminergic response in SUD patients that is functionally equivalent to, and synergistic with, pharmacological stimulant use.
* **Hypothesis 2 (The Liquidation Trap):** SUD patients exhibit a significantly lower threshold for "sunk cost fallacy" when interacting with AI credit-burn systems, leading to a measurable acceleration in financial extraction compared to a neurotypical control group.
* **Hypothesis 3 (Dark Triad Correlation):** Startups that score highest on the "Extractive Architecture Index" (a metric of dark pattern density) are statistically more likely to be led by founders scoring highly on Dark Triad personality assessments and funded by firms utilizing extreme Blitzscaling mandates.

## 3. Methodology

### Phase I: Qualitative Data Parsing (Powered by Claude)
* **Data Sources:** Anonymized user telemetry, forum posts (e.g., Reddit communities for AI tools), and direct qualitative interviews with individuals identifying as having both an active stimulant addiction and high-frequency AI usage.
* **Analysis:** Utilizing Anthropic's Claude to parse vast datasets of natural language to identify behavioral markers of hyper-focus, technological stress, and financial distress.

### Phase II: Architectural Deconstruction
* **System Audits:** Reverse-engineering the UI/UX flows of top generative AI platforms (e.g., voice cloning, image generation) to map the "disloyal design" elements.
* **Friction Analysis:** Measuring the exact implementation of "Spite-Utility" features (rate limits, unprompted quality throttling) and correlating them to known triggers for emotional dysregulation in SUD patients.

### Phase III: Leadership Profiling
* **Psychological Mapping:** Analyzing public communications, corporate histories, and platform policies of top AI executives through the lens of the "Dirty Dozen" Dark Triad scale.
* **Financial Modeling:** Tracing the funding rounds and VC mandates that pressure these executives into deploying hostile architectures.

## 4. Expected Deliverables
A comprehensive, peer-reviewed grade report detailing the mechanisms by which AI capitalism exploits neuro-vulnerability, providing a framework for clinical intervention and digital regulatory policy.
